OUR MISSION

The mission of The Nesting Place is to foster personal safety, growth, and self-sufficiency in women who are suffering from the effects of domestic violence, human trafficking, and sex trafficking. Since 2020, The Nesting Place assists survivors in finding emergency and permanent housing; along with programs to begin the healing process. Our Resources page contains materials that aim to educate survivors and the general public. If you or a loved one is in need of assistance through The Nesting Place, visit our Client Info section for contact methods.
